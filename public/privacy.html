Privacy Policy

Chandra - Indian Moon Tracker

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Chandra provides Panchang, Tithi, Nakshatra, moonrise, moonset, eclipse, and Hindu festival information for Indian cities. Most calendar and astronomy calculations run on your device. We collect limited data only when it is needed for settings, analytics, or optional push notifications.

Information We Store On Your Device

Chandra stores your app preferences in your browser's local storage so the app can remember your choices. This may include your selected city, latitude, longitude, language, calendar system, notification preferences, and whether you chose GPS-based city detection.

This local information stays on your device unless you enable a feature that needs server support, such as push notifications.

Location

Chandra uses your city to calculate local Panchang timings, moonrise, moonset, Rahu Kaal, and festival observance windows.

If you select a city manually, Chandra stores that city and its coordinates locally. If you tap "Use my location," your browser may ask for location permission. Chandra uses that location to find the nearest supported city. The precise browser location is not stored by Chandra after that matching step.

If push notifications are enabled, the selected city and city coordinates may be saved in Firebase so alerts can be calculated for the correct location.

Push Notifications

If you allow push notifications, Chandra stores a device notification token in Firebase Firestore. We also store your selected city, latitude, longitude, calendar system, notification preferences, active/inactive status, update time, and notification deduplication fields.

This data is used to send alerts you enabled, such as festival alerts, eclipse alerts, moonrise reminders, and Ekadashi or Pradosh observance alerts. You can turn notifications off in Chandra's Settings or in your browser/device notification settings.

Analytics

Chandra uses Google Analytics to understand basic app usage, such as page views and events like city changes or notification preference changes. Analytics helps us improve the app and diagnose product issues. Analytics providers may process technical information such as device, browser, approximate location, and usage data according to their own policies.

Third-Party Services

Chandra uses third-party services to operate the app:

  • Firebase Firestore, Cloud Functions, and Firebase Cloud Messaging for notification tokens, preferences, scheduled alerts, and backend processing.
  • Google Analytics for app usage analytics.
  • Vercel for website hosting and delivery.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Show accurate local Panchang, moon, and festival information.
  • Remember your app settings.
  • Send notifications only when you enable them.
  • Maintain, secure, debug, and improve Chandra.
  • Comply with legal, platform, and safety obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help operate Chandra, such as Firebase, Google Analytics, and Vercel, or when required by law.

Your Choices

  • You can change your city, calendar system, and language in Settings.
  • You can deny or disable browser location permission.
  • You can disable push notifications in Chandra's Settings or in your browser/device settings.
  • You can clear local Chandra data by clearing your browser/site storage.

Data Retention

Local settings remain on your device until you change them or clear site storage. Notification device records may remain in Firebase for operational, security, deduplication, and compliance purposes. Inactive records may be retained so Chandra can avoid sending alerts to disabled devices.

Data Deletion Requests

You can request deletion of server-side Chandra data associated with your device, such as notification tokens, selected city, notification preferences, and inactive notification records.

To request deletion, use the developer contact email listed for Chandra in Google Play. Include a short note that you are requesting data deletion for Chandra. If possible, also include your selected city and the approximate date you enabled notifications so we can locate the relevant record.

You can also clear local Chandra data from your own device at any time by clearing browser or site storage for Chandra.

Children

Chandra is a general-audience Panchang and moon calendar app. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We use Firebase security rules, service-provider security controls, and limited data collection to help protect user information. No internet service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Chandra changes. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have privacy questions or data deletion requests about Chandra, use the developer contact information provided in Chandra's Google Play listing or app store listing.
